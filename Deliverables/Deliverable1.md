GE Aerospace is looking for a Software Engineering PLacement, who will work on avionic projects hasving tasks of analysis, application design,
 system monitoring and collaborate with stakeholders. Company seeks for a candidate who is related to computer science field, passionate about

Job role is suited for the recent graduates or students in computer science who will use programming languages like JAVA, C++, Python and other in 
software development, testing and making the program efficient through their skills. Candidate will receive mentorship, environment to work in dynamic team
and benefits from company along with the salary.

Other Skills:
